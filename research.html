Master's Study
Glad to share...

In my Master's study, I implemented Virtual Reality in Video Modelling to help autistic children adapt to a new primary school. The intervention includes customization features for the parents of autistic children, aimed at enhancing the realism of the virtual school and improving the children's immersion.
